Customer Engagement Events

Customer Engagement Events provide a local platform for the field sales team and our key supplier partners to engage customers, develop relationships and drive sales growth. These shows are fully managed and executed by our Pittsburgh events team and events fully utilize field supplier BDF funds. These events fill up fast and customers performance updates will now be included on the new loyalty platform under "Events."

Contact the Pittsburgh events team directly to schedule a show or reach out to your local region marketing manager and they will point you in the right direction.

Connect Plus

Who is eligible and how does it work?

  • Any WESCO customer that purchased less than $200,000 in 2019 is eligible for Connect
  • Purchases from January 1, 2021 through December 31, 2021 qualify
  • Only one customer contact can participate and redeem points earned
  • Over 35,000 reward options including merchandise, gift cards, and local event tickets are available via our online category ordering tool
  • Points are paid out at $50,000, $100,000, $200,000 and $300,000 customer purchase milestones

Customer Point Structure

  • Maximum value for any customer participant is 75,000 points
  • $50,000 in purchases receives 12,500 points
  • $100,000 in purchases receives an additional 12,500 points
  • $200,000 in purchases receives an additional 25,000 points
  • $300,000 in purchases receives an additional 25,000 points
Connect Plus Taxable Income

All WESCO Loyalty participants are responsible for taxable income. Once customers earn points they will be able to redeem those points on our online merchandise site. Before redeeming any points customers must provide an updated business or personal W9.

Details provided on the W9 form will be used to report the necessary taxable income value to the IRS. Note that WESCO is NOT required to report any taxable income value under $600.

Participants that provide incorrect W9 information will be removed from the WESCO Loyalty program and not eligible to participate in future programs.

Wesco VIP - 2020 Program Snapshot

Take the time to familiarize yourself with the VIP program rules. It is imperative that you understand how to qualify and how credit is earned. Please be sure to log into the platform frequently to view purchase information and key program information and news.

  • Program Dates: All sales included starting Jan. 1, 2020 through Dec. 31, 2020
  • The Purchase Goal: These are the purchases (sales dollars) you need to achieve in order earn a trip for two people. Each customer will be assigned 1 purchase goal for 2020.
  • The VIP Trip: A once-in-a-lifetime, 6-night trip to the beautiful island of Maui
  • Travel Dates: The VIP Program Travel will take place June 21 through June 27, 2021 with no exceptions. If you cannot travel on those dates there will be no awards in lieu of the VIP Program trip.
  • Supplier Bonus Multipliers. An incentive for our customers to purchase products from suppliers that are participating in VIP. Customers can earn up to a 10% bonus on select purchases. This information is available on the WESCO Loyalty Platform website.
  • Review your VIP purchase goal with your WESCO account manager so they can answer any questions you may have. Share current and future project needs and map out a plan to ensure that you achieve your goal.
  • Taxable Income: Let's face it, nobody likes taxes. But to remain compliant with the IRS, WESCO will need to request W9 information prior to travel. Providing an updated business W9 is mandatory prior to travel eligibility.
Connect: Merchandise Incentive Program

Who is eligible and how does it work?

  • Any WESCO customer that purchased less than $200,000 in 2019 is eligible for Connect
  • Purchases from January 1, 2021 through December 31, 2021 qualify
  • Only one customer contact can participate and redeem points earned
  • Over 35,000 reward options including merchandise, gift cards, and local event tickets are available via our online category ordering tool
  • Points are paid out at $50,000, $100,000, $200,000 and $300,000 customer purchase milestones

Customer Point Structure

  • Maximum value for any customer participant is 75,000 points
  • $50,000 in purchases receives 12,500 points
  • $100,000 in purchases receives an additional 12,500 points
  • $200,000 in purchases receives an additional 25,000 points
  • $300,000 in purchases receives an additional 25,000 points

Wesco Loyalty Taxable Income

All WESCO Loyalty participants are responsible for taxable income. Customers that achieve their WESCO VIP purchase goal will be eligible for group travel. During the travel registration process, customers must provide an updated business W9. Business details provided on the W9 form will be used to report the necessary taxable income value to the IRS. Note: WESCO is not required by the IRS to report any taxable value for businesses that are incorporated (i.e., S-Corp, C-Corp, etc.)

Participants that provide incorrect business W9 information will be removed from the WESCO Loyalty program and not eligible to participate in future programs.
