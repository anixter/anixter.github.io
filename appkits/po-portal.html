Customer Search Portal

Need additional information or want to request mockups, screens, or graphics? Get in touch and we can create what you and your customer need.

Description of Application

A search portal application that works with SX.e that gives the user to search for orders using a variety of different criteria.

Development URL: https://devwebapps.anixter.com/poportal/default.aspx

Production URL: Coming Soon

Features at a Glance

  • Desktop, mobile, and tablet support.
  • Web based application.
  • .NET backend with HTML/CSS/Javascript frontend.
  • Ability to search via entered date, shipped date, promised date, and invoice date.
  • Multiple different views of search results.
  • Ability to view details on individual orders
