EDI (Electronic Data Interchange)

Anixter is committed to extending the benefits of electronic connectivity across our supplier base. Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) allows you to collaborate electronically with us on order information, purchase orders, invoices and shipping notices. Our EDI systems decrease paperwork and offer fast and efficient ordering while greatly reducing the chance for error.

If you have EDI capabilities, but are not yet exchanging an 850 Purchase Order, 855 Purchase Order Acknowledgment and an 810 Invoice document with Anixter, please review our EDI forms sections below and contact us directly for detail EDI Testing Procedures.
