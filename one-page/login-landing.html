EDI (Electronic Data Interchange)

Anixter Power Solutions is committed to extending the benefits of electronic connectivity across our supplier base. This initiative is a critical component of our efforts to control the costs associated with handling paper transactions. Your support and understanding of the strategic value of EDI processing for both our companies will help make this a mutual success.

If you have EDI capabilities but are not yet exchanging an 850 Purchase Order, 855 Purchase Order Acknowledgment, and an 810 Invoice document with Anixter Power Solutions, please review our EDI forms sections below and contact us directly for detailed EDI Testing Procedures.
