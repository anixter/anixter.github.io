Welcome to Anixter Power Solutions

Anixter Power Solutions has spent time getting to know customers in the construction, industrial, public power and investor-owned utilities segments. Through building and cultivating those relationships and consistently providing customers with comprehensive solutions, our team becomes more than just familiar with their business - we become part of it.

As a trusted ally and consultant, we wake up each day to provide customers with comprehensive solutions for strengthening their business. From our robust technology platform to our ability to boost efficiencies in your process, our world-class electrical wholesale supply chain is entirely dedicated to driving performance up and costs down.

Whatever segment you're in, Anixter Power Solutions offers access to a vast selection of products from top-name suppliers. Our associates are skilled at sourcing hard to find and specialty items, and our individual supplier relationships ensure competitive pricing and quick turnaround time - leading to the peace of mind of having what you need, where you need it and when you need it.

EDI (Electronic Data Interchange)

Anixter Power Solutions is committed to extending the benefits of electronic connectivity across our supplier base. This initiative is a critical component of our efforts to control the costs associated with handling paper transactions. Your support and understanding of the strategic value of EDI processing for both our companies will help make this a mutual success.
