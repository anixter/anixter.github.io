About Us

HD Supply Power Solutions is now Anixter Power Solutions! More than just a distributor, we will continue to offer exceptional customer service, an extensive portfolio of products and customized solutions – but now with an expanded geographic footprint as we join forces with Anixter International, Inc.

As a part of Anixter Power Solutions, we are able to draw on our combined strengths to offer our customers an unparalleled supplier relationship. We have been serving electrical utilities and contractors across Canada and are proud to include among our clientele the Provincial Utilities, Municipal Electric Utilities, REAs and many private Utility Contractors.

Anixter Power Solutions is committed to our customers, and we have experienced Customer Service Representatives available to assist you. Let us show you our partnering approach - contact us today!
